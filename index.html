Role of the systems engineer in Colombia

OBJECT OF PROFESSIONAL ACTIVITY

The professional activity of the Systems Engineer is developed around Information systematically and mechanically processed. In other words, around that set of conventional symbols characters, images, sounds, appropriately structured, that immediately convey knowledge that has some value, or that can be used to perform an action, or to make a decision, and which is processed by using of a system that includes computers and telecommunications mechanized system. In other engineering companies, professional activity revolves around other natural resources or artificial, different from information, and made available to its users through systems other than Information Systems. For example, the professional activity of An electrical engineer works around electricity and employs electrical systems transmission lines, generators, transformers. Activity professional hydraulic engineer revolves around water, and uses hydraulic systems turbines, tunnels, tanks, dams.

Area of responsibility

The essential responsibility of the Systems Engineer is to properly satisfy the requirements of the users of their work, within the parameters of time, cost, etc., that are agreed. This naturally implies the execution of the operational work of letting available the product required by users, and the managerial work of complying with the parameters to be agreed.

Homework

Whatever the job responsibility of the Systems Engineer and the scope of the himself, some of the duties that differentiate him from the technologist, the technician and the operator are presented below. These, technologists, technicians and operators, are also part of the engineering team in charge of which is the work to be done. The Systems Engineer must deliver solutions that not only satisfy the requirements of users, but that they conveniently adjust to current technological, professional and industrial standards. These solutions must also fit appropriately in the set of goods or services of the user organization, so that it can fulfill your business or institutional goals, and the engineer's work contributes to progress community.
